Lake Ray Hubbard

Public Access Facilities

There is no camping around Lake Ray Hubbard, but Robertson Park is a large day-use area with excellent bank fishing access and two boat ramps. Ramps and other services are available at other city parks and private marinas around the lake. For more details, choose a point on the map or a name in the chart below.
